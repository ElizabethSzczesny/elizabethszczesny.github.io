Project Overview

This project was created in collaboration with Resilience Inc., a non-profit for Social Emotional Learning curriculums. The website, Mylearningtools.org, is intended to help teachers implement SEL curriculums in their classroom as well as educate about the organization's mission to improve mental health in K-12 schools.

Graphic Design

These graphics were created to represent the major themes in the SEL Education Games they represent: Quoted from Mylearningtools.org, "“Dealing with Conflict” is an engaging, educational game-play experience that challenges you to think about conflict resolution and tolerance, "Self Talk Jenga” is a social-emotional gaming activity designed to help a player with self-reflection, “Growth Mindset Maze” is a fun, easy, educational obstacle course game designed to challenge a players’ knowledge of growth and fixed mindsets, and "SELENA VR": Deep in the vast emptiness of space, you are suddenly awoken to the sound and image of your ship being hit by an asteroid storm! To get through this, you’ll need SELENA, as you navigate your feelings."

Design System

Leaning into the signature green color, and a complimentary bright color palette, gold highlights appear in minimalist linework. The green and gold are also represented in the Resilience Inc. logo.
