About & Contact

Elizabeth Szczesny is a UX/UI Designer and Web Developer based in Michigan. Her background in the arts is complimented by technical development knowledge. Drawing from this diverse skillset, she creates an online presence for businesses of all sizes, with a clean and elegant aesthetic.

Formally trained in Google's UX Design Specialization which teaches the 5-stage Design Thinking model proposed by the Hasso Plattner Institute of Design at Stanford. The stages of this Design Thinking process are as follows: empathise, define, ideate, prototype, test. In addition to being trained in design thinking I possess a Bachelor's of Arts in Studio Art, and am fluent in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I have worked extensively with Wordpress for clients.
