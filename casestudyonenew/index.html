Project Overview

The challenge of this project was to create a wedding planning application for a venue. The constraints users face when planning weddings are primarily those related to time. It is often difficult to manage planning a wedding on top of daily responsibilities and work. The aim of this application is to streamline the process and save the user time without having to sacrifice on a beautiful, individual experience and event.

Design Process

Wireframes

Addressing User Pain Points With Design

Those planning their wedding online are interested in the unique features venues have to offer and how the space looks, so this must be conveyed accurately. Both photo and video are offered to the user. By offering video the user can take a virtual tour of the venue - without needing to make an appointment - and thus saving time. There are also a multitude of high quality images of the venue in a slider format.

Addressing User Pain Points With Design

Another obstacle for those planning their wedding online is becoming overwhelmed with the task. This is addressed by designing an efficient planning interface.

The planning interface begins with clearly stated directions. This one spreadsheet is designed for complete planning with minimal steps:

Check date availability

Make selections for vendors

Choose the number of guests

Then, use the cost estimate calculator. This calculator can be used as many times as needed to configure different choices. Once your decisions are made you have the option to book the selections, make an appointment, or save the selections to an account.

Addressing User Pain Points With Design

The navigation items are drawn up, on both the top and bottom, in individual blocks. These blocks visually demonstrate all the major components that go into planning, aiding in organizing the users' mind whilst planning.

Finally, an easy access point to ask questions is listed at the bottom of every page.
