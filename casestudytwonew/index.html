Project Overview

This project was creating a delivery tracking flow for a fictional logistics company. When tracking a package, especially internationally, consumers prefer as much information, regarding the whereabouts and timeline of delivery, as possible. The aim of this application is to efficiently provide these details in a reader-friendly format.

Research Survey Participant

Amazon is the best in terms of tracking in my opinion because they tell me when its going to ship, where it is at any given time, and what stage it is up to- and every package no matter the cost is tracked. Ally Express in the worst in my opinion because there is never any tracking information. I get an email sometimes saying it has arrived in my country, but there’s no timeframe attached to that. So it could arrive in my country, and be another week or two weeks, and there is no way to know. Plus, I can’t see if it’s left a given country to estimate when it’s going to arrive.
